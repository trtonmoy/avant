ALL ABOUT US

Avant Smart Card

Step back into the transformative 1990s with Avant, the Bank of Finland's pioneering venture into the world of Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC). Avant, the first-ever CBDC, aimed to replace coins for small purchases through a national electronic purse system. Though initially operated by the central bank, Avant's journey saw it evolve, eventually being spun off and sold to commercial banks as newer technologies emerged. Witness the brief yet impactful era when Avant set the stage for the digital financial revolution.
